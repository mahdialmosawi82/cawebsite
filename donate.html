Support CreateAccess

Thank you for considering a donation to help us broaden access to 3D creation! Donations bring microcourses to more schools and community programs, and help us build new microcourse content for beginners. ... Please contact Executive Director Liz Dailey at liz@createaccess.org if you have questions, if you are considering a gift of $10,000 or more, or if you would like to discuss recommending a grant to CreateAccess through your Donor-Advised Fund. CreateAccess is currently registered to accept charitable contributions from residents of Pennsylvania and Maryland. Arizona, Delaware, Idaho, Indiana, Iowa, Montana, Nebraska, South Dakota, Vermont, and Wyoming do not require registration to accept charitable contributions. If you are a resident of one of the states not listed here and wish to support CreateAccess, please contact Liz Dailey prior to making a donation so the necessary registration can be completed, if any.

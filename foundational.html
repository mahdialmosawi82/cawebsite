About this Course:

This course provides resources to help creators who are new to 3D creation learn basic skills, tips, and tricks to get started with some of the 3D creation tools used in CreateAccess microcourses. You don't need to review this content to complete a microcourse, but it can help you complete microcourses and experiment with your own projects more quickly and easily.
